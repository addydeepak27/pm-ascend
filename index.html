FAQ

Questions we get a lot

If yours isn't here, use the Contact link in the footer and we'll get back to you.

Do I need technical experience to sign up? ▼
No. The platform is built for people coming from all backgrounds — engineering, marketing, finance, teaching, and more. Your analysis and roadmap are generated based on your specific profile, so someone with zero technical experience gets a different plan than a software engineer. The goal isn't to make everyone an engineer; it's to show you what AI knowledge you actually need for the role you want.
What does an AI Product Manager actually do? ▼
An AI PM owns the product strategy for software that uses artificial intelligence — things like recommendation systems, chatbots, image recognition tools, or AI-powered features inside larger apps. The role is the same as a traditional PM (define what to build and why, work with engineers and designers, measure outcomes), but requires enough understanding of how AI models work to make smart product decisions — like knowing when an AI approach is the right tool, or how to handle model failures gracefully.
How is my roadmap generated, and is it truly personalized? ▼
Yes. When you upload your resume or answer the questionnaire, our AI analysis engine examines your specific background — your role, industry, tools you've used, education, and target role — and generates a roadmap tailored to your gaps. A software engineer targeting consumer AI gets a completely different plan than a finance analyst targeting enterprise AI tools. The more detail you provide, the more specific the roadmap.
How long does it take to land an AI PM role? ▼
It depends on your starting point and how much time you invest. Based on our alumni data: engineers typically land roles in 5–9 months, data scientists in 5–8 months, business analysts in 6–8 months, marketing and operations backgrounds in 6–9 months, finance backgrounds in 9–11 months, and students or career changers in 7–11 months. The timeline your analysis generates is calibrated to your specific background — we don't believe in false promises. Consistency matters more than intensity.
What is a cohort, and do I have to join one? ▼
A cohort is a small group of 15–20 people on similar AI PM journeys, led by a mentor who is a current or former AI PM. Cohorts meet weekly for live problem-solving, resume reviews, mock interviews, and accountability check-ins. You don't have to join one — the free Starter plan includes 1 cohort session per month so you can try it — but most people find it's the single biggest accelerator. Pro members get uncapped cohort access.
Is the free plan genuinely useful, or is it just a preview? ▼
The free plan gives you a full AI resume analysis, a personalized roadmap, a readiness timeline, 5 practice problems per month, 1 cohort session, and access to our resource library. That's genuinely useful — many people go from zero clarity to a personalised, week-by-week plan just from the free analysis. The Pro plan unlocks 1-on-1 coaching, uncapped practice, mock interviews, and the job referral network — which matter more once you're actively interviewing.
Can I get an analysis without a resume? ▼
Yes. The dashboard has a Quick Profile option — you answer 10 questions about your background, industry, AI tools you've used, and what kind of AI PM role you want. The analysis is just as specific. You don't need a polished resume to get started.
Can I cancel my Pro plan at any time? ▼
Yes, cancel anytime with no penalty. You keep access until the end of your billing period and then drop to the free Starter plan. No contracts, no cancellation fees.
