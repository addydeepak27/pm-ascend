FAQ

Questions we get a lot

If yours isn't here, use the Contact link in the footer and we'll get back to you.

Do I need a technical background to use this? ▼
No. The platform is built for people coming from all backgrounds — engineering, marketing, finance, teaching, and more. Your analysis and roadmap are generated based on your specific profile, so someone with zero technical experience gets a different plan than a software engineer. The goal isn't to make everyone an engineer; it's to show you what AI knowledge you actually need for the role you want.
What exactly is an "AI Product Manager"? ▼
An AI PM owns the product strategy for software that uses artificial intelligence — things like recommendation systems, chatbots, image recognition tools, or AI-powered features inside larger apps. The role is the same as a traditional PM (define what to build and why, work with engineers and designers, measure outcomes), but requires enough understanding of how AI models work to make smart product decisions — like knowing when an AI approach is the right tool, or how to handle model failures gracefully.
How is the roadmap generated? Is it really personalized? ▼
Yes. When you upload your resume or answer the questionnaire, Claude (Anthropic's AI) analyzes your specific background — your role, industry, tools you've used, education, and target role — and generates a roadmap tailored to your gaps. A software engineer targeting consumer AI gets a completely different plan than a finance analyst targeting enterprise AI tools. The more detail you provide, the more specific the roadmap.
How long does it realistically take to land an AI PM role? ▼
It depends heavily on your starting point and how much time you invest. Based on our alumni data, most people land roles between 6 and 14 months. Engineers with some product exposure tend to be at the shorter end; people with no tech background or PM experience typically take 12–18 months. The timeline your analysis gives you is an honest estimate — we don't believe in false promises. Consistency matters more than intensity.
What's a cohort, and do I have to join one? ▼
A cohort is a small group of 15–20 people on similar AI PM journeys, led by a mentor who is a current or former AI PM. Cohorts meet weekly for live problem-solving, resume reviews, mock interviews, and accountability check-ins. You don't have to join one — the free Starter plan includes 1 cohort session per month so you can try it — but most people find it's the single biggest accelerator. Pro members get unlimited cohort access.
Is the free plan actually useful, or is it just a teaser? ▼
The free plan gives you a full AI resume analysis, a personalized roadmap, a readiness timeline, 5 practice problems per month, 1 cohort session, and access to our resource library. That's genuinely useful — many people go from zero clarity to a clear 6-month plan just from the free analysis. The Pro plan unlocks 1-on-1 coaching, unlimited practice, mock interviews, and the job referral network — which matter more once you're actively interviewing.
What if I don't have a resume? Can I still get an analysis? ▼
Yes. The dashboard has a Quick Profile option — you answer 10 questions about your background, industry, AI tools you've used, and what kind of AI PM role you want. The analysis is just as specific. You don't need a polished resume to get started.
Can I cancel the Pro plan anytime? ▼
Yes, cancel anytime with no penalty. You keep access until the end of your billing period and then drop to the free Starter plan. No contracts, no cancellation fees.
